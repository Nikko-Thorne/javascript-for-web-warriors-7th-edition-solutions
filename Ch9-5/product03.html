Glomitts

Our Glomitts represent the best of both worlds. The mitts fold back to allow you to work with your fingers for maximum dexterity. After your work is through, slip the mitt hood back over your fingers for warmth and comfort. The Glomitt is made from a blend of 75% wool and 25% synthetic fabric for extra comfort, warmth, and durability.
